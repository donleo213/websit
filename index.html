Welcome to the Al-Quds Youth Leadership Academy

A New Generation of Leaders Begins Here!

At the Al-Quds Youth Leadership Academy (YLA), we merge the discipline of competitive soccer with the power of public speaking, critical thinking, and holistic education. Our mission is to equip young minds with the confidence, skills, and resilience they need to thrive—both on and off the field.

Are you ready to lead, think, and play at your highest potential?

About Us

Who We Are

The Youth Leadership Academy (YLA) is more than a sports program—it’s a transformative leadership journey. We believe true champions are defined not just by their performance on the field, but by their ability to think critically, communicate effectively, and lead with confidence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a generation of bold, intelligent, and resilient young leaders by integrating competitive soccer, public speaking, critical thinking, and holistic education. Through this unique approach, we empower youth to build discipline, teamwork, and excellence while developing the skills needed to excel beyond the game. At YLA, we prepare young individuals for professional success and leadership roles, shaping well-rounded champions ready to make an impact.
